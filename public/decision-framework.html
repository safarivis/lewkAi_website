AI Adoption Decision Framework

Run every AI investment through this checklist before committing resources. Based on the Six Pillars of AI Velocity.

First Principles Check
What fundamental customer problem does this solve?
What can this technology verifiably do today?
What is our unique ability to deliver value?
Articulation Check
Have we extracted the implicit requirements — the 95% the client hasn't said?
Are we building what the client needs, or what they asked for?
Base Rate Analysis
What % of companies that adopted this saw measurable improvement?
What is the typical failure rate and why?
Cost-Benefit Model
Estimated costs vs benefits (probability-weighted)?
Break-even timeline?
Pre-Mortem
If this fails in 6 months, what are the 3 most likely reasons?
What evidence in 30/60/90 days would show it's not working?
Simplicity Check
Is this the simplest viable approach?
Are we rebuilding or bolting on?
TDD Check
How will we test that this is working?
What are the measurable success criteria?
Value Check
Does this let us charge for value, not headcount?
Will this compound — more valuable in 12 months?
Decision
Proceed
Investigate Further
Reject
